About

My name is Amy Frear and I live in Philadelphia. My background is in film, performance, and academia. When the pandemic hit, I found myself with scary feelings and a lot of extra time. So I began experimenting with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Playing around with code put me in a much better mood. Now I am studying to become a developer.

My goals:

  1. Learn more languages
  2. Build a new career path
  3. Integrate these skills with the work I already do in video and performance

Stay Tuned for Projects!

Film

I create short films that explore subtle science fiction and fantasy through getting lost, connection, romantic notions, theatricality, bright colors, and silliness overcoming aggression and control.

Acting

I've worked in independent film and in professional theater across the country. I have my MFA in Acting from the University of Washington and I am an adjunct professor of acting at Temple University and the University of the Arts.

Performance Art

I perform with visual artist Iva Yos. Our interests include silliness undercutting aggression and neuroticisms in a capitalist culture. We also like talking softly into our microphones and depicting jokes with large scale drawings.
